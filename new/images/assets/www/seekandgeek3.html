SEEK AND GEEK 3: WALKING MECHANISMS!

My seek and geek this week are link mechanisms. Link mechanisms are great, simple ways to achieve complex motions with a single degree of freedom. The first mechanism is involved in walking. There are multiple ways to achieve walking motion with linkages. The Klann mechanism is a 6-linkeage mechanism to achieve this motion.


Jansen's linkage is an 8-linkeage mechanism to achieve a similar walk motion. The Klann mechanism is cheaper due to fewer links and can work on uneven ground.
