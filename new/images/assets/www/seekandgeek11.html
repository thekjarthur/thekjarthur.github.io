SEEK AND GEEK 11: Wing Corkscrew

I recently saw a corkscrew being used and I though about how interesting it was. It uses a worm and worm gear in a very interesting way. When the handle is rotated, the screw digs in to to cork. Since it is a work, it rotates the gear attached to the wings. Since worm gears aren't backdrive-able, pushing the wings down will cause an upward force on the worm, rather than a reverse rotation.
