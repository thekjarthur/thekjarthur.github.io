Practical Uploadable Problem Set #9

Peer Reviewers: Maria Ruiz, Akwasi Owusu-Akyaw

Bearings and actuators are good enough for the final model. The bearing support will need to be improved upon; there will be addition parts screwed in below the block to provide structural support.

The final dimensions for the subsequent components are all in the "dimensions" tab of the error-apportionment.xlsx. The FRDPARRC table hasn't changed though the error-budget spreadsheet has seen some update.

The table is safe to operate. There was an issue with the motor controller overheating. This issue has been rectified. After building it the first time, I would be able to iterate and build a second desk in half the time, remedying most of the issues that were found during the first build.

Brainware and Hardware

The full design of the machine is documented here. My MCM was my dovetail slider and actuation system. The dovetails were cut into the 2-by-8 with the center slab used as the carriage. I made sure to match the sides. To minimize misalignment, the bearing blocks are situated in the bearings instead of attached at the ends. A spider coupling will be used to mitigate large misalignments with the motor.
