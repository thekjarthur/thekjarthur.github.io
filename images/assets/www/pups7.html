Practical Uploadable Problem Set #7

Peer Reviewers: Maria Ruiz, Akwasi Owusu-Akyaw

The bearings will be a simple slider bearing with preloading and low friction pads at each corner.

The system is gravity preloaded. The leadscrew however will be put in tension before loading. The bearings will also be preloaded by cutting cantilevers into the journal.

Preloading will decrease the live of the bearing due to wearing from the friction.
