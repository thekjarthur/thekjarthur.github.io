2.70 FUNdaMENTALS OF PRECISION DESIGN

This course examines design, selection, and combination of machine elements to produce a robust precision system. It introduces process, philosophy and physics-based principles of design.

Final Project

This year, I will design, manufacture and reiterate in order to create a desk capable of transforming for a standing and sitting user. The whitepaper can be accessed here. The entire design process and final documentation can be found here.

Coursework

Classwork was assigned weekly. Problem sets are under the "PUPS" section. The theoritical/machine-shop tasks are under "BRAINWARE" and "HARDWARE". The "SEEK AND GEEK" section contains gadgets, mechanisms and inventions I thought were interesting.
