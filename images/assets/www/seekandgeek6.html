SEEK AND GEEK 6: Flywheel Energy Storage

One idea to ensure constant power supply is to store up energy when it is cheap and demand is low and dispense it during high demand. Chemical systems typically can't handle large capacities and are limited by speed of storage and discharge. Mechanical systems are the bests method. Flywheel energy systems work by storing energy in a high speed heavy flywheel. These flywheels are spun in vaccuum environments and have low friction magnetic bearings.
