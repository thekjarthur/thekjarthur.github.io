  • Low Photon Count Phase Retrieval Using Deep Learning

    Alexandre Goy, Kwabena Arthur, Shuai Li, and George Barbastathis - Physical Review Letters.

    doi:10.1103/PhysRevLett.121.243902

    Article on MIT.edu

    We combined knowledge of physical imaging models, with a deep learning approach for phase retrieval under extremely photon-starved conditions. We also show negative Pearson Correlation Coefficient (NPCC) to be very effective as a loss function.
